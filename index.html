1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Identity: Full name, profile photo
  • Contact: Phone number, email address
  • Address: Saved service and delivery addresses
  • Location: GPS coordinates when selecting a service location
  • Media: Photos of devices or repair issues you upload
  • Payment: Transaction references only - not raw card data
  • Usage: App interactions, crash reports, device type
  • Notifications: FCM push notification tokens
We do NOT store raw card numbers, CVVs, or bank details. All payment processing is handled by Razorpay under PCI-DSS compliance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and process repair bookings
  • To show your service address on the map and assign nearby technicians
  • To initiate and verify payments via Razorpay
  • To send booking confirmations and status updates via push notifications
  • To provide customer support and resolve disputes
  • To improve app performance using anonymised analytics
  • To comply with Indian laws and regulations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only:

  • With assigned technicians - name, phone, and service address to fulfil your booking
  • With service providers - Firebase, Razorpay, Google (see Section 4)
  • For legal compliance - if required by court order or government authority
  • On business transfer - in a merger/acquisition; you will be notified

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: Data retained while your account is open
  • After deletion: Personal identifiers deleted within 30 days; anonymised booking records retained up to 7 years for legal/audit purposes
  • Uploaded photos: Deleted within 90 days of account deletion
  • Server logs: Anonymised logs retained up to 12 months

6. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2+
  • Firestore security rules enforce per-user access control
  • Firebase Authentication manages credentials - we do not store passwords
  • Payment data handled exclusively by Razorpay (PCI-DSS Level 1)
No electronic system is 100% secure. We implement reasonable safeguards but cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe your child has provided us data, contact us immediately and we will delete it.

8. Your Rights & Account Deletion

Under Indian law and our policy, you have the right to:

  • Access - request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification - update your profile in-app at any time
  • Erasure - permanently delete your account and data
  • Withdraw Consent - for non-essential data use, contact us
  • Grievance Redressal - raise concerns with our Grievance Officer (IT Rules 2011)
Account Deletion: Open the app - Profile - Privacy Policy - tap "Delete My Account". This immediately revokes authentication, permanently erases personal data, and anonymises booking history. This action is irreversible.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes in-app and update the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance.
